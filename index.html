Background

I am currently a Ph.D candidate at the department of Mechanical engineering and Material science, Duke univeristy, majoring in Medical Robotics. I received my BS degree from Central South University, Changsha China, with a major in Thermal Energy and Dynamic Engineering. My research interests are Sensor Fusion and Calibration, Medical Robotics, 3D Reconstruction and Registration, Model Predictive Control and Optimization-based Planning, Machine Learning.
