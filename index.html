Guangshen Ma

I am a Ph.D candidate at Duke University majoring in Mechanical Engineering and Robotics. I obtained my BS degree frmo Central South University (Changsha China) with a major of Thermal Energy and Dynamic Engineering. My current research interests are Robot Perception (Machine perception and 3D computer vision), Optimization-based motion planning and Machine Learning for medical data analysis. You could find more information in my Linkedin [link]

Research Projects
