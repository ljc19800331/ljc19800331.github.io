Research Projects

This page contains a list of research projects.

  • Ma. G., Ross, W., Codd, P. Robotic Laser Orientation Planning with a 3D Data-driven Method (Submitted to ICRA 2022).


    • The first demo shows the major objective of the paper. The aim is to design a motion planner to find the optimal laser orientation angle to achieve a pre-defined surgical objective, such as avoid overcutting healthy tissue by the laser.


    The second demo shows deformation of each point robot agent by using the proposed planning strategy. The point robot agent can be controlled to a "safe region" after the planning procedure, and keep the distance from the obstacle boundary.


    The third video show the validation results from simulation.



  • Ma. G., Ross, W., Codd, P. StereoCNC: A Stereovision-guided Robotic Laser System, submitted to 2021 International Conference on Intelligent Robots and Systems (IROS 2021), (Accepted). [pdf]


    • This demo shows the complete setup of stereovision-guided robotic laser system.


    This demo shows the human-in-the-loop image-guided laser surgery process.



  • Ma, G.*, Oca, S*. (Equal contributed), Zhu, Y., Codd, P. and Buckland, D. A Novel Robotic System for Ultrasound-guided Peripheral Vascular Localization, 2021 International Conference on Robotics and Automation (ICRA 2021). [pdf]


    • This demo shows the automated vein tracking system with the accepted paper. The complete procedure is fully automated.


    This demo shows the 3D vein reconstruction with the robot-controlled ultrasound image data.



  • Research project: Robotic laser system for automated 3D image-guided surgery.


    • This demo shows geometry model of the optical beam path propagation.


    This demo shows geometry model of the galvo mirror.
